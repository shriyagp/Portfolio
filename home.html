Sweet Crumbs - Social Media Campaign

Building a community-driven brand presence for a local bakery

Problem

Sweet Crumbs, a small local bakery, needed to increase its social media presence and build customer loyalty among young adults and families. The challenge was standing out in a crowded market while maintaining authenticity.

Process

Target Audience Research: Identified two key personas - Riya (23, college student) and Arun (29, young professional) - who value Instagrammable food and meaningful discounts.

Campaign Strategy: Developed #CrumbsOfHappiness campaign encouraging customers to share photos with their treats in exchange for discounts. Created a content calendar balancing promotional posts, engagement content, and user-generated content.

Visual Identity: Designed consistent pastel-toned social media posts with a friendly, approachable aesthetic that resonates with the target demographic.

Solution

Launched an integrated social media campaign featuring:

  • Weekly content calendar with polls, quotes, festive offers, and UGC highlights
  • Instagram Reels showing behind-the-scenes baking process
  • Targeted paid ads (Buy 1 Get 1 offers) for weekend sales boost
  • Community engagement through hashtag campaign

Results

Hypothetical Analytics:

  • 30% increase in engagement rate
  • 250+ new followers monthly
  • ‚Çπ10,000 revenue from paid ads
  • 4.5% CTR (above industry average of 2%)
  • Increased brand trust through authentic UGC content

Vedic Tattva - EdTech Digital Marketing

Blending ancient wisdom with modern learning through strategic digital marketing

Problem

Vedic Tattva needed to establish itself as a credible EdTech platform that uniquely combines traditional Vedic methodologies with modern subjects. The challenge was reaching both students struggling with conventional learning and parents seeking value-driven education.

Process

Brand Identity: Created a visual identity featuring lotus flame symbolism, blending orange (energy) and blue (trust) colors with clean UI for accessibility.

Multi-Channel Strategy: Developed #LearnWithWisdom campaign featuring weekly educational reels, carousel posts blending Vedic math shortcuts with modern techniques, and informative content for parents.

SEO & Analytics: Implemented SEO strategy targeting keywords like "vedic maths online" and "holistic learning," while running Meta ads to drive sign-ups.

Solution

Launched comprehensive digital marketing campaign including:

  • Weekly content calendar with educational reels, carousels, polls, and parent-focused posts
  • Targeted Meta ads with strong CTAs for course enrollment
  • SEO optimization for organic reach
  • Student testimonials and UGC for community building

Results

Hypothetical Analytics (14-day campaign):

  • Google Analytics: 950 sessions, 800 new users, 1m 10s avg. session
  • SEO: Domain score 11/100, 120 organic visitors/month, 7 keywords ranked
  • Meta Ads: 8,500 impressions, 320 clicks, 3.76% CTR, 34 sign-ups, 2 paid enrollments
  • 7% conversion rate with 4.3% ROI
  • 30% increase in engagement, 20% increase in parent engagement
